About Me

Hi, I am Shahid from Dhaka, Bangladesh. Designing & Developing websites is my passion. I love Coffee & Coding. When I am not coding I read books, blogs and watch Cricket matches. I also do a little bit of blogging.

Web Development

HTML & CSS

I learnt HTML5 & CSS3 about 2 years ago. I love to follow the W3 standard for clean and valid markup. For responsiveness of the website I use Bootstrap framework. I use SASS preprocessor for my CSS compilation.

JavaScript

I have started learning JavaScript about six months ago and still going. I know jQuery and now learning the AngularJS framework. I also know a little bit of Node JS.

PHP

I know the basics of PHP. I have used the Laravel framework for one of my client project. I also used the WordPress CMS for some of my blog which I developed from scratch.

Web Design

Ideology

I follow the minimalistic idelogy. I believe less is more. It is also a trend now-a-days. For Single Page Application (SPA) I like to follow the Google's Material Design pattern.

Design Methodology

I love pen & paper very much. First I brainstorm on the given idea and jot down all the features. Then I research for the current trends and scetch wireframes on paper. Finally I convert all the wireframes into PSD designs.

Tools I Use

I use Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator for my web design work. I also use GIMP & Inkscape sometimes. Google Fonts is my primary source of fonts & I use FontAwesome for Icons.
